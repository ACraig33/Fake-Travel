Top Historical Destinations

1. Rome, Italy

Why Visit:
Rome is a truly special place. With its rich history and gorgeous architecture, breathtaking churches, fountains, and piazzas it has something for everyone. Whether you are looking for delicious food, fabulous shopping, or exciting entertainment, you will find it all in Rome Why Visit: Rome is a treasure trove of ancient history, with monuments, ruins, and buildings dating back thousands of years.
Must-See:
The Colosseum, Roman Forum, Pantheon, Vatican City (St. Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel), and the ancient city of Ostia Antica.

3. Istanbul, Turkey

Why Visit:
Istanbul has a very rich history. You can witness the fertile history from the Ottoman and Byzantine periods. You will discover the city that was once Byzantium and called Constantinople and was the crown of three empires. Places such as Sultan Ahmet Mosque, Dolmabahçe Palace, Hagia Sophia, Spice Bazaar and Süleymaniye Mosque are places to be seen at least once in a life. A city that straddles two continents, Istanbul has a fascinating history shaped by Roman, Byzantine, and Ottoman empires.
Must-See:
Hagia Sophia, Topkapi Palace, Blue Mosque, Basilica Cistern, and the ancient Hippodrome.

Why Visit:
The little country is absolutely jam packed with historical sites that are worth seeing whether you practice Judaism, Christianity, Islam, or no religion at all. Jerusalem alone is full of architecture dating back to the time of (and before) Jesus, all in one small historic quarter of the city.
